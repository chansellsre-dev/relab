Agency Relationships (Quick Hits)

  • Fiduciary duties: Obedience, Loyalty, Disclosure, Confidentiality, Accounting, Reasonable care.
  • Types: Seller agency, Buyer agency, Dual agency (disclose & get consent), Designated agency.
  • Listing agreements: Exclusive right-to-sell, Exclusive agency, Open listing, Net listing (avoid).

Contracts

  • Essential elements: Offer, Acceptance, Consideration, Legal purpose, Competent parties.
  • Contingencies: Financing, Appraisal, Inspection, Home sale.
